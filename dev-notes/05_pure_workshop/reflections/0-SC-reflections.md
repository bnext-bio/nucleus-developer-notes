# Day 2 - protein mix reflection
Sonication seems to be a point that needs to be re calibrated for each equipment and each experimental setup in a lab. Our sonicator is a QSonica125, for lysates I haven't had great results because I still need to calibrate the lysis steps. However for the sonication of recombinant proteins (approx 40mL of culture) here is the protocol that we use, with a thin probe:
1s ON - 4s OFF
40% amplitude
Time ON: 4-8mn according the protein (20-40mn total). We adjust the sonication time by searching for a change in the density of the lysate, often associated with a change of color (slight).
I don't have the amount of equivalent energy, I could write it down next time. 
I liked the use of gravity columns, much simpler than any fplc or even the peristaltic pumps we use. And way faster --> is that good though? Residence time of the buffers and the lysate in the column/resin?

Dialysis steps were helpful. I wasn't super comfortable with the use of Amicon and spin tubes for dialysis, now I have manipulated of all sizes :) It was interesting to calculate the amount of imidazole in each step. 


# Day 3 - rescue the protein mix by drop ins
I was a bit suprised by the issues we had with the Pierce660 kit, specially regarding Jake's results (twice). I'm not sure what was going here. 

The drop in experiment was actually great. It took us a while to design it, but I really feel that we were very careful in designing the controls and the different conditions, so that the experiment was actually useful, I mean interpretable. It has happened to me countless times that because of wanting to do something quicky I neglect the design and then I don't know if my experiment is actually answering the question I had. So I really feel that it was time that was worth investing. Now regarding the data it is difficult to assess how useful the supplementation was, however there was a tendency where several conditions of supplementation actually improved the expression of the reporter. IF2 seemed to play a relevant role and in some cases a two-fold increase was observed. Although the overall activity is marginal comparting to the commercial control, it still gives a hint. Now, ideal case would be to assess the statistical significance of the differences observed but it's not possible with so few points.
